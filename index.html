Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Discover quick solutions and helpful tips for a seamless shopping experience.

1. How do I place an order online?

Answer:
Placing an order is simple! Browse our online catalog by category or use the search bar to find specific items. Add your chosen products to the cart, review your order, and proceed to checkout. We offer multiple payment options—including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and secure online banking—to ensure a smooth and safe transaction process.

2. What delivery options are available, and how soon can I receive my order?

Answer:
We offer a range of delivery options to fit your schedule: Same-Day Delivery: Order before our cutoff time to receive your groceries the same day in select areas. Scheduled Delivery: Choose a convenient time slot that works for you. Express Delivery: For urgent orders, select our express service where available. Delivery times and fees may vary based on your location and order size. Check our Delivery Information page at checkout for detailed estimates.

3. How do I track my order once it’s been placed?

Answer:
After your order is confirmed, you’ll receive an email and/or SMS with a unique tracking link. Simply click the link to view real-time updates on your delivery status. You can also log into your account on our website to access the “My Orders” section for a complete history and current tracking details.

4. What is your return and refund policy?

Answer:
We strive to ensure you’re completely satisfied with your purchase. If you receive damaged or incorrect items, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement, refund, or credit according to your preference. For more details on eligible items and the return process, please visit our Return Policy page or reach out to our support team.
